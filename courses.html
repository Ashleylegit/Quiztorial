adjust your quiz mode

Practice

Description: Allows users to take quizzes without any time pressure or scoring.
‍Benefits:
- Encourages exploration and learning without the stress of performance.
- Provides an opportunity to review material and identify areas for improvement.
- Enables users to experiment with different answer choices and strategies.

Timed

Description: Challenges users to complete quizzes within a set time limit.
‍Benefits:
- Simulates the pressure of real-world exams and assessments.
- Improves time management skills and decision-making under pressure.
- Provides a sense of accomplishment upon completing the quiz within the time limit.

Adaptive

Description: Adjusts the difficulty of questions based on the user's performance.
‍Benefits:
- Provides a personalized learning experience that caters to individual strengths and weaknesses.
- Keeps users engaged and challenged by presenting questions that are neither too easy nor too difficult.
- Helps users identify areas where they need additional practice and support.

challenged

Description: Presents users with more difficult and complex questions than in other modes.
‍Benefits:
- Provides an opportunity for advanced learners to test their knowledge and skills.
- Encourages critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.
- Offers a sense of accomplishment and intellectual stimulation.
